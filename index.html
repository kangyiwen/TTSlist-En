Introduction

chatTTS is a lot of fun, but I spent a lot of time drawing voice cards, so I just generated them all and shared them with everyone.

The following 1000 voices start from 100, with a step of 100, all the way to 100000. Currently, there is no distinction between male and female voices. Choose the voice number you like and enter it into your chatTTS parameter to produce it. If the voices are still not enough to play with, please let me know.

Additionally, the English version of the voice library is in production...
